# PSSUQ-Based Usability Questionnaire for FSMES

## Purpose
This questionnaire is used to gather participant feedback on the usability of the Faculty Scholarship Grant Management and Evaluation System (FSMES) after the assigned system tasks have been completed.

This instrument is based on the Post-Study System Usability Questionnaire (PSSUQ) framework described in Chapter 3 of the study. The item wording below is adapted to the FSMES prototype and organized according to the three usability dimensions used in the methodology:
- System Usefulness
- Information Quality
- Interface Quality

## When to Administer
Administer this questionnaire after:
- the participant completes the assigned FSMES tasks
- functionality testing has already been conducted
- the participant has experienced the workflow features relevant to their role

## Intended Respondents
- Faculty applicants
- Academic Scholarship Panel member

## Response Scale
Please rate each statement using the following scale:

| Score | Response |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Strongly Agree |
| 2 | Agree |
| 3 | Slightly Agree |
| 4 | Neutral |
| 5 | Slightly Disagree |
| 6 | Disagree |
| 7 | Strongly Disagree |

Note: Lower scores indicate more favorable usability perceptions.

## Instructions to Respondents
Please answer all items based on your actual experience while using FSMES during the test session.

- There are no right or wrong answers.
- Focus on how usable, understandable, and appropriate the system felt during your assigned tasks.
- If an item does not apply exactly to your role, answer based on the closest part of your experience.

## Researcher Scoring Note
- Compute the mean for System Usefulness using Items 1 to 8.
- Compute the mean for Information Quality using Items 9 to 14.
- Compute the mean for Interface Quality using Items 15 to 18.
- Compute the overall mean across all questionnaire items.
- Interpret lower mean scores as more favorable usability perceptions, consistent with Chapter 3 of the study.
